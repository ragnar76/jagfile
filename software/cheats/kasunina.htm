WINNING THE GAME

When you defeat Gyaku on Hard or Ninja God levels only, DO NOT touch him when it says "show no mercy". DO NOT perform a Death Move. Gyaku will transform into a Demon for the final battle in Hell. Defeating Gyaku in Hell will close the portal.

SECRET STUFF

CHARACTER VS. CHARACTER CHEAT

This cheat enables you to play against the same character (Angus vs. Angus, for example). One character appears slightly darker than the other. This cheat requires two controllers.

  • At the OPTIONS screen, select "change code"
  • Hold down A+C on controller #2
  • Enter the code 621544 on controller #1

You will hear an audible "ha" and see "wrong code" on the screen. Now play the game and select your characters. After you select a character, he/she will not disappear, enabling player two to select that character also.
