DEFENDER 2000

  • Easter Eggs:
    • Plazma Pong: Enter "Nolan" in any high score table to get an extra game
    • Flossie's Revenge: Enter "Ovine" in Defender Plus high score table. Start Defender Plus by pressing A (instead of B).
    • Warp Enable: Enter "Beest" in Defender 2000 high score table. During Defender 2000, press 3 to warp to the next level, and press 6 to warp to the bonus round.
