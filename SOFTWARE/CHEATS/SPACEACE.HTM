SPACE ACE

General Tips:

Timing is critical, a move made too early or too late will end your mission. Usually you should only press your move once. While playing through some scenes however, you may press your move many times before the action in order to insure the execution of the move. Several scenes may appear flipped, left to right. For these scenes, substitute left for right, and right for left in the following hints.
